KillQuest is a pulp [adventure game](http://questingblog.com/adventure-game-vs-osr) about assembling a cult of colourful characters; each with their own beliefs, motives and struggles trying to complete questionable jobs in the crumbling remains if a once mighty empire. You should expect lethal skirmishes, faction based politics, high stakes heists and player-driven campaigns.

Within this document, you will find the essential rules to enhance your storytelling experience. Don't let the rules dominate your actions, instead use them to facilitate captivating narratives, inspire intrigue, and ensure the game flows smoothly.
