KillQuest is an [adventure game](http://questingblog.com/adventure-game-vs-osr) where you command a cult of colourful characters, each with their own unique skills, agendas and stories. Set against the backdrop of the crumbling remains of a once mighty empire, it’s a game of deadly engagements, covert missions, high-stakes objectives, faction based politics and player-driven campaigns.

Within this document, you will find the essential rules to enhance your storytelling experience. Don't let the rules dominate your actions, instead use them to facilitate captivating narratives, inspire intrigue, and ensure the game flows smoothly. 

Welcome to KillQuest, a fantasy adventure game where you and your company of colourful characters must try and survive in the crumbling remains of a once mighty empire.
