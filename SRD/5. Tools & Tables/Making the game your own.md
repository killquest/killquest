We have created a setting from the ground up to work with KillQuest rules and playstyle. We believe it is the best way to start your first campaign. There is enough detail, and recognisable tropes to make you feel at home, but enough is unfamiliar and challenging that it shouldn't feel played out and boring.

That being said creating your own characters, histories and events is really what makes the world feel alive and something you can be proud of. 

We have plenty of tables and tools to create a living and thematic world. Some players prefer to write up a few well perfectly thematic characters before they play, others roll on the tables and create more unpredicatable procedurally generated characters with their own wacky charm.

We designed KillQuest to be a frictionless fiction, that isn't bound to a particular genre or style of play. These are basic rules, which you can add extra modules to create more flavourful and custom settings, or keep things basic to improve accessiblity.
