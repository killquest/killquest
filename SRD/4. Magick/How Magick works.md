You roll three dice, each success on the dice enables a more powerful effect 

Each spell haw a power rating, if you fail to cast the spell correctly then you must roll on a critical magick chart. You must add the power rating plus a d6 wild (meaning if your roll a 6, roll again) and find out where the effects land on the chart. Much like the wild magic chart from D&D but more powerful magic has the potential to go very wrong.

You roll an opposed roll of your [[divine]] against the difficulty of the spell, so 3 difficulty spells will at best have a 50% chance of succeeding and a much greater chance of going wrong with it going drastically wrong if it does.
##### Wizards Magick
This comes from three parts;
- Quality of 
- Their ability to [[divine]] magick
- Their spell experience level

##### Spell Difficulty
Spells can become easier to cast as you cast them, brining their difficulty down. Also certain spells are made easier to cast by using certain relics.

##### Magick Items
For Magick items, the three values are;
- Metal they are made from
- Gems quality to [[divine]] magick
- Their spell experience level
- Magick items are rated as uncommon, rare & legendary. Uncommon items can be found on people occasionally, can be ordered with next day delivery in the capital, three days in a city and add on travel distances to the village.
- Rare items can be purchased, but are very expensive. They usually end up in the hands of the rich and wealthy and occasionally have a chance to turn up in treasure hoards. 
- Legendary items can only be attained through quests or taking them from another character. These are great hooks to get players into the game once they start playing
