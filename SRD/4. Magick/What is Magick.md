Magick, it is the power to alter reality. It twists the fabric of understanding, and is powered by the belief that it exists. It is a chaotic and unpredictable force that can be both a boon and a curse to those who wield it.

##### How it works
Magick is powered by **the collective subconscious of sentient creatures**, because of this it is highly influenced by superstitions and beliefs. This means that if enough people believe something to be true, it can actually become true. 

##### How do I use Magick?
Since **you play as a Djinn** you can control the flow of magick. Although without a physical form you can only cast magick through your followers, and how powerful the spell is, is determined by their ***divine*** ability.
##### Divine Powers
You are playing the role of a divine power, trying to raise a cult. Not every character is equal in their ability to divine what you want want them to do, which causes a [[magick misfire]]. 
##### Casting
Critical - You can use two effects
Success - You can use one effect
Failure - You can use one effect, and you must roll twice on the magick blunder chart.

##### Do I need a Wizard?
Technically not, anyone can use magick. However wizards understand the secrets of channeling your divine will. They have the right equipment and they look the part, so people expect them to perform it well. Since they are the closest to you, they are the hardest to replace.
1. Take [[mushtea]] everyday for a month
2. Cut out an eye and replace it with a gem
