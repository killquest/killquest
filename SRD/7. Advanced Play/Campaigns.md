Campaigns require Game Masters or Tournament Organisers. They play like Risk, but fighting over resources, bonuses etc. whilst also getting better at killing monsters etc like root. You choose your faction and how you want to dominate the board.


##### Casual
once a week on the campaign level everyone comes together to make their moves, and resolve any conflict and reveal interactions. 

##### Ongoing
Best played online or you see the people your playing with daily. Have daily challenges, and open conflict can happen on any day.
