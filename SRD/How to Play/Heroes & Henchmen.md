### Heroes
You start with one hero, and as you complete quests and help locals, your reputation grows, making it easier to recruit more powerful Heroes. 

### Henchmen
It's never too hard to find henchmen, and they are fairly easy to replace. You can go to a hotel in the city or town, to resupply henchmen. They are easier and cheaper to recruit from friendly factions.
