In the Mistborn series, magic is known as Allomancy, and those who can wield it are called mages. Allomancy is based on the ingestion and "burning" of different metals to gain specific magical abilities. There are three categories of mages:

Mistings: These individuals can burn a single type of metal, which grants them a particular magical ability.
Mistborn: A rare few are Mistborn, capable of burning all the known metals and thus possessing a wide range of magical abilities.

The 16 Metals:
The magic system is built around 16 different metals, each with its own unique ability when burned by an mage:

##### Iron
can create a bubble of time around them, slowing down time for everything inside, allowing the mage to experience time more slowly than the outside world.
##### Steel
Allows mages to push or pull nearby objects, granting them a form of telekinesis
##### Tin
Enhances physical senses, such as sight, hearing, and smell; becoming more aware of your surroundings.
##### Pewter
Increases physical strength and endurance, making you more resilient and powerful.
##### Zinc
Manipulate the emotions of others, influencing their feelings.
##### Brass
Detect the use of other Magick abilities in their vicinity
##### Copper
Provides the ability to hide magick from detection
