### When coming from existing popular RPG’s
- ***Virtually no maths.*** We have removed maths from wherever it is possible, and when it does occur it's rarely more complicated than 6+5.
- ***You don't control a single character.*** You will be roleplaying a roster of misfits. You play as a god, guiding and influencing a cult of your followers, some unknowingly.
- ***Democratic rulings.*** You don't need a DM, and decisions are to be agreed by a majority at the table. Although I'm sure a good many groups will unanimously decide to return to the old ways and put everything on a Dungeon Master.

- ***A TTRPG with no forced roles.***  Everyone takes on the same role as a player, there is no DM, and you control multiple characters, meaning no-one has to play as a healer for the whole team. 
- ***Pre-determined Randomness.*** We limit what is possible before you do it, rather than finding out after. This means less disappointed players, and rewards clever thinkers and problem solvers.
