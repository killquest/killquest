##### Heroes
Heroes are the protagonists of our stories. Most stories are generated by the Heroes, either from their past or through their actions.

You start with one hero, and get more by saving a NPC's life, or rescuing people. You roll your ***reputation*** bonus to see if you can ***recruit*** them.

They live in your hub, and you know their lives. They fight, and will most likely die for your cause.

Hirelings are hired guns, they can fill out specialist rolls, although they can get costly if you want them to be highly skilled. 

Here are a few potential mechanics for recruiting hirelings that could add some interesting twists to your game:

##### Recruiting Heroes
Heroes are recruited by spending their reputation points. As they complete quests and help locals, their reputation grows, making it easier to recruit more powerful Heroes who are eager to join a group giving them the chance to earn glory and progress in their career. 

##### Recruiting Henchmen
It's never too hard to find henchmen, and they are fairly easy to replace. You can go to a hotel in the city or town, to resupply the squads. They will be out of action whilst they retrain new recruits.

###### Hirelings
Players can buy the services of different hirelings, each with a specific set of skills and abilities. The more powerful and useful a hireling is, the higher their price will be.

1. Hireling Loyalty: Make hirelings more than just disposable mercenaries. Give them personalities, goals, and motivations that can impact their loyalty to the party. Players will need to manage relationships with their hirelings, keeping them happy, and fulfilling their desires to maintain their services.
2. Aspiring Adventurer System: Allow players to recruit potential adventurers who are looking to join a party but lack the experience or skills needed to do so. These characters could start at lower levels or have limited abilities, but as they gain experience and complete quests with the party, they could level up and become valuable members of the group.
3. Hireling Factions: Create different factions of hirelings with different goals and values. Some might be interested in accumulating wealth, while others might be more focused on exploring ruins or defeating monsters. Players will need to choose which faction to recruit from and manage relationships with different factions to keep them all happy.
